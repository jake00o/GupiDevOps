Frequently Asked Questions

What causes back pain?

Back pain can result from poor posture, injuries, sedentary lifestyles, or medical conditions. Identifying the root cause is essential for effective treatment.

How long do sessions take?

Each session typically lasts 45 minutes to 1 hour, ensuring you receive comprehensive attention and care.

Do I need to bring anything?

Wear comfortable clothing that allows for movement. No special equipment is necessary; we'll provide any tools needed.
